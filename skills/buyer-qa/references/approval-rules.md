# Approval Rules

Buyer replies are drafts only.

Do not send replies automatically.

## Required Log State

Each draft must include:

- `Status: awaiting_approval`
- `Action: Approve / Edit / Discard`

## Commitments Need Approval

Lucas must approve before committing to:

- lower price
- counteroffer
- pickup time
- reservation
- shipping exception
- full pickup address
- refund / complaint remedy

## Full Address

Do not reveal the full address until buyer commitment.

Safe public/general wording:

```text
Abholung ist in 3075 Rüfenacht möglich. Die genaue Adresse senden wir nach fixer Zusage.
```
