# Stale Item Rules

Apply only when dates are available in local status files.

## Fixed-Price Listings

- Older than 7 days: consider lowering price by 15%.
- Older than 14 days: consider cross-posting or refreshing.
- Older than 21 days: consider bundling, concrete box lot, or price reset.

Do not lower prices automatically.

## Drafts

- Draft older than 2 days with missing information: flag for Lucas.
- Draft approved but not posted: flag for posting.

## Q&A

- Awaiting approval over 24 hours: flag as priority.
- Pickup or offer messages should be higher priority than generic detail requests.

## Email Inbox

- Unmatched marketplace email: flag for listing URL / item ID reconciliation.
- Parsed but not handed to `buyer-qa`: flag for processing.
