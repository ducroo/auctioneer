# Platform Rules

Available now:

- Ricardo
- tutti
- Anibis
- Vinted
- Facebook Marketplace

Planned later:

- Ron Orp
- Autoscout24
- eBay for special cases only

## Ricardo

Strengths:

- broad Swiss reach
- high trust
- binding auction-style sales
- good for liquid, branded, vintage, collectible, and valuable items

Weaknesses:

- success fees
- more listing discipline needed

Use for:

- valuable single items
- branded tools and electronics
- vintage items with broad appeal
- complete quality sets
- items likely to attract multiple bidders

## tutti

Strengths:

- free
- strong for local pickup
- simple fixed-price listings

Weaknesses:

- less binding
- more flaky buyers

Use for:

- bulky pickup-only items
- furniture
- rugs
- large tools
- concrete box lots
- low to mid-value local items

## Anibis

Strengths:

- useful backup/cross-posting platform
- broader Swiss reach
- stronger in Romandie than some Swiss-German defaults

Weaknesses:

- may have lower local traction in Swiss-German regions

Use for:

- backup if tutti fails after about 14 days
- bulky/local pickup cross-posts
- items where French-speaking reach may help later

## Vinted

Strengths:

- best fit for clothing, shoes, and accessories
- built-in clothing marketplace expectations

Weaknesses:

- low margins
- haggling

Use for:

- clothing
- shoes
- wearable accessories
- clothing bundles

## Facebook Marketplace

Strengths:

- fast local reach
- useful for bulky pickup items

Weaknesses:

- flaky buyers
- more noise and negotiation

Use for:

- fast local pickup
- bulky low/mid-value items
- items where speed matters more than price certainty

## eBay

Strengths:

- international reach
- useful for specialist/lightweight collectibles

Weaknesses:

- weaker domestic Swiss default
- shipping and disputes can be annoying

Use for:

- lightweight specialist items
- collectibles with international demand
- rare parts or model-specific objects

Avoid as a normal Swiss domestic marketplace.

## Future Platform Notes

- Ron Orp: aesthetic/design-conscious urban pieces, special rugs, traditional Swiss furniture with visual appeal.
- Autoscout24: the two cars only.
- eBay: international/specialist exception only.
