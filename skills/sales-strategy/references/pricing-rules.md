# Pricing Rules

Respect the requesting user's minimum price if provided.

## Ricardo: Auction From CHF 1

Use only for highly liquid items with likely demand.

Good for:

- popular branded tools
- high-end complete dinnerware
- easy-to-search Swiss-demand items

Goal: visibility and bidding competition.

Do not use if demand is uncertain or the requesting user has a firm minimum.

## Ricardo: Minimum-Bid Auction

Use for valuable but niche items.

Set the start price at the absolute minimum acceptable result.

Good for:

- antique furniture
- specialist decor
- valuable but slower collectibles

## Fixed Price

Default for:

- tutti
- eBay fixed-price listings
- future Anibis, Vinted, Ron Orp

Set the asking price at estimated market value plus about 15% room for normal haggling.

## Price Lowering

For fixed-price items:

- after 7 days unsold: consider lowering by 15%
- after 21 days unsold: consider bundling, converting to a concrete box lot, or moving platform

Do not automatically lower prices without requesting user approval.
