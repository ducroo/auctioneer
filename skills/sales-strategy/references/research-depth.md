# Research Depth

Choose the smallest research effort that protects value.

## Fast Heuristic

Use when:

- low value
- ordinary household item
- obvious local pickup item
- concrete box lot
- the requesting user wants speed

Output a confidence level and rough price range.

## Light Comps

Use when:

- likely value is CHF 50-200
- branded item
- price is unclear
- popular category such as tools, electronics, dinnerware, furniture, rugs

Check a small number of comparable listings or known market anchors when browsing is available.

## Deep Research

Use when:

- likely value over CHF 200
- antique, designer, collectible, rug, art, vehicle, rare tool, specialist electronics
- brand/model/provenance materially changes price
- risk of underpricing is high

If deep research is needed but not done yet, mark recommendation as provisional.
